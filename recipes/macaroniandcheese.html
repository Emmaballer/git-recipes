The Best Macaroni and Cheese You'll Ever Try

If you're anything like me, you probably liked macaroni and cheese a lot more as a kid than you do now. But alas, I've found it: the perfect macaroni and cheese for adults. Seriously, this puts Kraft to shame. Crispy golden brown cheese complete with al dente noodles drenched in gooey sauce.
It resembles something like a casserole, but for the busy bodies out there, like me, this will be perfect for you. Set it and forget it. Then eat it. What's not to like? Scroll for recipe.
Ingredients

  • 1 lb dried elbow pasta
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 2 1/2 cups half and half
  • 4 cups shredded medium cheddar cheese
  • 2 gups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 tbsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp. paprika

Let's Make It!
  1. Preheat your oven to 325 degrees F and grease a 9x13" baking dish
  2. Boil the pasta and cook for 7-9 minutes, not quite al dente
  3. While the pasta is cooking, mix the cheese and toss. Divide into three piles.
  4. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Sprinkle flour and combine. Cook for one minute, whisking often. Slowly pour in 2 cups of the half and half and whisk until smooth. Pour in the rest of the half and half and the milk, and combine until smooth
  5. Heat over medium heat until thickened.
  6. Remove the mixture from heat and stir in spices and cheeses.
  7. Mix pasta and cheese sauce. Pour half of the pasta mix into the baking dish, then top with 1 1/2 cups of shredded cheese. On top of that, pour the remaining pasta mixture. Top with remaining cheese.
  8. Bake for 15 minutes, until cheese is golden brown. Let cool and serve.
