Not Your Gramma's Tikka Masala

This has to be my favorite Indian dish. Buttery, tender chicken over fragrant, fluffy rice; this recipe will have you on your knees. Scroll for recipe.


Ingredients for Marinade
    28 oz (800g) boneless and skinless chicken thighs cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon ginger
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon tumeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon Kashmiri chili
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

    • Ingredients for sauce
    • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
    • 2 tablespoons butter
    • 2 small onions
    • 1 teaspoon brown sugar
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1 1/4 cups of heavy cream
    • 4 tbsp fresh cilantro
    • 1 tsp kashmiri chili
    • 1 tsp ground chili powder
    • 14 oz tomato puree
    • 1 tsp ground coriander
    • 1 1/2 garlic finely grated
    • 1 tsp ginger finely grated
    • 1 1/2 tsp garam masala
    • 1 1/2 tsp ground cumin
    • 1 tsp tumeric

    1. In a bowl, combine ingredients and coat chicken well. Marinate for atleast an hour, or ideally overnight.
    2. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat and add chicken pieces in small batches. Fry until browned.
    3. After cooking the chicken, melt the butter. Fry the onions until soft.
    4. Add garlic and ginger and saute. Then add garam masala, cumin, tumeric, and coriander. Fry for 20 seconds until fragrant.
    5. Mix tomato puree, chili powder and salt. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally until sauce becomes brownish red.
    6. Stir cream and sugar into sauce. Add chicken and back into pan and cook for 10 minutes.
    7. Reduce heat, serve. Garnish with cilantro. Best served over a bed of jasmine rice and naan bread!
